Electron-Electron Correlation in Vertically Stacked Nanorings

Marlon Rincón Fulla

Resumen


 

 

Spatial correlation effects for two electrostatically coupled electrons in two vertically stacked narrow nanorings with different radii have been analyzed. The energy levels as a function of the ratio between rings radii, the interring distance and the magnetic field, applied along the common axis, are found and analyzed. As the ratio between rings radii tends to one or the distance between rings tends to zero, our results coincide with those results previously obtained for two identically and vertically separated nanorings as well as the two-electron case constrained to move in a single one-dimensional ring.

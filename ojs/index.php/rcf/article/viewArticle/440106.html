An Accidental Relationship Between a Relative Quantum Phase Distribution and Concurrence

Rubén Darío Guerrero Mancilla, Rafael Ramón Rey González, Karen Milena Fonseca Romero

Resumen


We consider some particular states of two coupled continuous systems, for which it is meaningful to use the concurrence as an entanglement quantifier, and show there is an accidental connection between the probability distribution of the relative phase of their Husimi function and their concurrence. We moreover show a quantum open system wherethese states can be produced. Hence, the measurement of the Husimi function allows for quantification of entanglement for these particular states.
